Biography

Linus Trovalds can software is a Finnish Ameengineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux kernel.Which is the kernel for a number of operating system. He also created the version control system git. Trovalds was born on December 28, 1969 in Helsinki,Finland. He studied computer science at the university of Helsinki. And he developed the Linux kernel as a student project in 1991.Since then,the Linux kernel has become one of the most widely used kernels in the world. Powering everything from smartphones to supercomputers.

Achievements

  • 1991-Created the Linux kernel version control system Git.
  • Named one of the "100 more influential people in the world" by time magazine in 2004.
  • Recieved an honorary doctorate from the university of Helsinki in 2010.
  • Recieved the 2015 IEEF computer society computer pioneer award.
  • Recieved the 2019 ACM SIGOPS Hall of fame award.
